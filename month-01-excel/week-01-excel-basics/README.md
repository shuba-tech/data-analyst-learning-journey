## What I Learned
Today I learned how to work with a large dataset (9,994 rows) in Excel. I practiced SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT and COUNTA. I learned the difference between 
COUNT and COUNTA — COUNT works for numbers only, COUNTA works for text like Order IDs. I also learned IF, COUNTIF and SUMIF formulas to answer real 
business questions.
